About Me

Festus Asante comes from Ghana in the West of Africa. He's the first son of Rev. Paul Asante & Naomi Asante. Festus had his first diploma certificate in Computer Networking Engineering and undergraduate degree in Computer Scieence at the Kwane Nkrumah University of Science & Technology, Kumasi in Ghana in the year 2020 and 2022 respectively. He's currently pursuing his Masters degree in Applied Computer Science & Cybersecurity at Grand Valley State University in Allendale, Michigan in the United States of America. Festus has a strong interest in System Analysis & Design and Cybersecurity.
